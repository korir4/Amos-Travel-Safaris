About

Amos, the CEO of the company is a passionate environmentalist and a winner of several global awards both for the firm and personal recognition for excellent conservation of nature.

We are a company that works towards encouraging environmental preservation for the good of our planet and its sustainablity. Through facilitating tours and having people truly experience the beauty, it gives everyone a sense of responsibility towards taking care of the flora and fauna.

What we do

We offer camping facilities, touring vans and personal tour guides at a negotiable and affordable fee for both local and international clients. Amos Safaris also collaborate with the government of kenya through kws and ministry of tourism towards achieving vision 2030.
